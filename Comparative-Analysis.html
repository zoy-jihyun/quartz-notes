“How can we provide personalized user experiences in shared mobility services using SDV and AI technologies in a fully autonomous (Level 5) future?”

  1. In the future, car ownership is expected to decrease, and autonomous shared mobility, where strangers share rides like public transportation will become more common.
  2. If you own a car, it’s completely yours, so you can personalize it to your taste, like decorating the interior or listening to your favorite music.
  3. But with shared mobility, that kind of personalization isn’t really possible. So I thought what if we could use SDVs(Software Defined Vehicles) and AI technology to create personalized experiences within shared mobility services?

Expected Output: Mobile UXUI + In-car experience(infotainment system)

This study compares two distinct layers of the user journey: the in-car experience system, represented by M.VICS 5.0, Tesla Model 3 (2025), and MBUX; the service-level experience (MaaS), represented by Uber; and an integrated shared mobility platform, represented by MOIA, combining both in-vehicle experience and service-level integration.

Five criteria: Context Awareness, Multi-User Handling, Interface Adaptability, AI Integration, Ambient Experience Design
